Academic Departments

Computer Science

Symbolic Anthropology

Students investigate the role of ritual, symbol, and belief in human societies. Emphasis is placed on the continuity of ritual language, semiotic preservation, and cultural continuity across deep time.

Students study memory, perception, identity, and decision-making through scientific and philosophical approaches, ensuring deep cognitive continuity.
